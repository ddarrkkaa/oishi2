Introducing The Koyo
Berry

Our newest varietal, the Koyo Berry, means “elated” in
Japanese. This berry has a slightly firm texture, bright aroma,
an eye-opening flavor, and a refreshing sweetness.

The Omakase Berry

A delicate sweetness, aromatics that fill the room, and a
deliciously creamy texture. A chef's favorite berry.

The Koyo Berry

A refreshing sweetness, bright aroma, subtle tartness, and
slightly firm texture. Perfect for the whole family.
